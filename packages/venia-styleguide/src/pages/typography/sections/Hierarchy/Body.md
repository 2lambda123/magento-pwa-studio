### Body

Use body styles for blocks of text throughout components.

***

#### Font size

Size  | Scale Value
:--- | :---
XXL | `fontSize-500`
XL | `fontSize-400`
L | `fontSize-300`
M | `fontSize-200`
S | `fontSize-100`
XS | `fontSize-50`

***
