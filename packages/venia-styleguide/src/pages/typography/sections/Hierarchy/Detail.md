### Detail

Use detail styles for labels and short descriptors.

***

#### Font size

Size  | Scale Value
:--- | :---
XL | `fontSize-200`
L | `fontSize-100`
M | `fontSize-75`
S | `fontSize-50`

***

#### Line height
