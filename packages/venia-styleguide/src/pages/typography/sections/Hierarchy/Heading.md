### Heading

Use heading styles to establish hierarchy through typography.

***

#### Font size

Size  | Scale Value
:--- | :---
XL | `fontSize-600`
L | `fontSize-400`
M | `fontSize-300`
S | `fontSize-200`
XS | `fontSize-100`

***
